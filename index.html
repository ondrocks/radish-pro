Radish-pro combines various information channels (email, VoIP, LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook) and provides means for a team of specialists to work with the same resources (the addressbook, projects etc.).

Radish-pro delivers dedicated solutions based on fast PHP5 webapplication development frameworks and has the best possible usability as a goal.

The database can be hosted on the corporate network and last but not least: by publishing the webapplication as a widget you can have it all on your handheld! That makes radish-pro the solution of 2011.
